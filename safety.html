Public Safety Alerts

Typhoon Alert

September 14, 2024

A severe typhoon is approaching the area. Residents are advised to stay indoors, secure their properties, and monitor local news for updates. Emergency services are on high alert, and shelters will be open at designated locations.

Flood Warning

September 13, 2024

Heavy rains have led to rising water levels in low-lying areas. Please avoid driving through flooded streets and stay in elevated areas. Emergency teams are conducting rescue operations in affected zones.

Public Health Advisory

September 12, 2024

There has been an increase in flu cases. Residents are advised to get vaccinated, practice good hygiene, and seek medical attention if experiencing symptoms. The local health center is offering free flu shots for the next two weeks.

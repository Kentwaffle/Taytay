Events

HAMAKA Festival

October 1 - October 7, 2024

The HAMAKA Festival is an annual cultural and arts festival held to celebrate the local heritage and traditions of Taytay. Enjoy various cultural performances, art exhibitions, and traditional Filipino games throughout the week.

Foundation Day of Taytay

September 30, 2024

Join us in commemorating the Foundation Day of Taytay, Rizal. The celebration will include a parade, historical exhibits, and community activities to honor the town's rich history and growth over the years.

Legal Holidays

2024

Here is a list of the legal holidays observed in Taytay, Rizal for 2024:

  • New Year's Day: January 1, 2024
  • Holy Week: March 28 - March 31, 2024
  • Independence Day: June 12, 2024
  • Ninoy Aquino Day: August 21, 2024
  • Bonifacio Day: November 30, 2024
  • Christmas Day: December 25, 2024
  • Rizal Day: December 30, 2024
